Estatio

Estatio Open Source Estate Management built on Apache Isis

About

Estatio is modern and flexible property management software. It offers real estate professionals and service providers the power and flexibility to manage their business in a superior, flexible and cost-effective manner.

Standing on the shoulders of giants

Estatio uses Apache Isis, a framework for rapidly developing domain-driven apps in Java.

Open Source

Estatio is Open Source sofware: you can adapt or extend the software to fit your needs.

Support or Contact

The team behind Estatio has proven for decades to deliver outstanding software and successfully implement property management software. Contact us at info@estatio.org if you need our help or just want to chat.
